Syria

In 2010, I traveled to Syria and walked the streets of the old city of Damascus. Here I found one of the largest and oldest mosques in the world. The Umayyad Mosque.

Ecuador

Guayaquil is the largest city in Ecuador, and also the Nation's main port. The chicken is delicious, there's the grand Metropolitan Cathedral, and the famous 'Iguana Park'!

Ottawa

A city rich in culture with absolutely no shortage of green space! Check out the nearby Gatineau Park in Quebec, the Rideau Canal, and pictured above Hog's Back falls.

About Me

My vision to help create an enlightened society. In order to acheive this I must examine my own experience to find what is of value. I will strengthen the connections with myself, my communities, and the earth, while sharing the lessons I learn along the way with others around me.

My purpose in life is to see, learn, and experience as much as I can. And then translate the feelings associated with these experiences into creative expression. My camera is a vital tool in this mission. The images I capture help me to see things in a different light. Perspective is everything.
